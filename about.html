About This Site

My childhood home had one magical room.

Floor to ceiling shelves were filled with science fiction books, radio and software guides, various batteries, and electronics. A tall rack metal rack was home to four gray computer towers, a printer, and a scanner. A single monitor in the middle of a wooden desk was my window to the early days of the internet. Through the late 90s and the 2000s, I grew curious and obsessed with everything the internet had to offer.

This site is a love letter to those times when the web felt new and sparkly.
